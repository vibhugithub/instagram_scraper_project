How to Download Instagram Media?



1. Copy Post's Link

Go to Instagram, open the Post which you want to download, and copy its link.

2. Paste Post's Link

Paste the post's link into the search bar at the top, and then press the Photo button if the post is a Picture or press the Video button if it is a Video.

3. Download Post
